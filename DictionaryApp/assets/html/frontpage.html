Cebuano Dictionary for Android

This is pre-release software under development, and may contain many bugs! Please contact Jeroen Hellingman if you have remarks, comments, or bug-reports.

To search, enter a Cebuano word in the search box.

This dictionary uses a strictly phonetic spelling of Cebuano. This differs from the orthography normally used in Cebuano publications. The letter o is always spelled as u and e is always spelled as i. The search takes this in account. Spanish and English loan-words are always written as pronounced by Cebuano speakers. Please read the introduction for more details on spelling and other features of this dictionary.

The settings screen allows you to change your preferences. You can change the font-size, as well as the presentation of articles and expansion of abbreviated forms. When you use the reverse lookup option, please realize this dictionary was made to look-up Cebuano words, not English words.

The content is based on A Dictionary of Cebuano Visayan by John U. Wolff. The full text of this dictionary is available from Project Gutenberg. A free searchable online version is available on www.Bohol.ph.
